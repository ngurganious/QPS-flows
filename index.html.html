QPS Platform — User Flow Diagrams

The QPS Platform is a clickable, role-aware Next.js 15 (App Router) prototype built for Optum Health's Quality and Patient Safety team. It centralizes safety incidents (eventually routed to Origami / Safety Event Reporting System), Quality measures (eventually sourced from Snowflake), and the resulting RCAs, action plans, signals, and MD&A responses. Six roles are supported: Submitter, Investigator, Closer, Business AQL, QPS Admin, and QPS ADL Team. A separate Clinical Sandbox under /clinical/* is an isolated digital-logs prototype that can be deployed independently.

Tip: click any sidebar journey to jump · arrows are labeled with the click/action that triggers them.
